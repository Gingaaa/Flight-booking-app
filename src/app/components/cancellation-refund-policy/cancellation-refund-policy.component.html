Cancellations and refund policy

For a little price, you can cancel your flight reservation on Sprucefare's website without any inconvenience. Before you decide to cancel your flight ticket, we urge you to study the Cancellation Policy of the relevant airline. Read on to understand all the factors better.

Flight Cancellation Policy

  • Any airline typically provides a window of 24 hours following the passenger's flight reservation. The airline may not charge a cancellation fee if you cancel your flight reservation within the first 24 hours of making the reservation.
  • There are some tickets that are not refundable. These tickets won't be refundable or subject to cancellation. To avoid last-minute disappointments, it is preferable to verify the ticket restrictions when making reservations.
  • There will be a cancellation fee if you want to cancel a ticket that is part of a package that also includes hotel accommodations, vehicle rentals, tour services, etc.
  • The cancellation cost may always change depending on the flight's destination, place of departure, booked fare class, time of cancellation, etc.
  • The airline will charge the whole amount if you don't cancel the flight ticket and don't even join the aircraft.

Refund Policy

  • If you decide not to go, you must cancel your reservation at least 24 hours before the flight is due to depart in order to receive a refund (applicable on refundable tickets). Please be aware that you will only receive the refund after paying any necessary cancellation fees. The refund and cancellation policies are also subject to change without prior notice
